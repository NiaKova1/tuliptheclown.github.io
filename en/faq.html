FAQ

Why do we recommend booking 2 entertainers for 20 plus children?

If there are a lot of children it will be difficult to pay attention to each child. Two entertainers can do a better job with large groups of children. We want to make sure that each child is happy. (We have a second entertainer).

Do you have a DBS check and PLI?

Yes I do

What age groups do you work with?

From 1 to 15 year olds

What costume is best for toddlers?

For toddlers we recommend the Clown and the Fairy costumes.

How much does it cost to book a children's entertainer?

The price depends on the amount of hours you are booking the entertainer for and the tube zone the party is in.

Do you take bookings outside of London?

Yes. There is an extra fee for travel cost and travel time (if it is more than 1.5 hours one way).

Do you do face painting and balloon modeling? Is it included in the price?

Yes. This is included in the price. Face painting takes around 5-10 minutes per child.

What games do you bring?

The entertainer brings a suitcase full of games, including:

  • magic show
  • puppet show
  • treasure hunt
  • musical games
  • relay races
  • arts and crafts
  • face painting
  • balloon modeling

You can choose which games you would like or we can choose the perfect games to suit the amount of children and what your child likes.

Do the children get prizes?

Yes. The entertainer brings stickers as prizes. All the children get stickers and the birthday boy/girl are always in the center of attention.
